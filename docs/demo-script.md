# LumenFi Demo Script

## 90-second walkthrough

1. Open `https://lumenfi.click`.
2. Connect a wallet on Arc Testnet.
3. Show the dashboard overview: balances, markets, bridge, docs, and deployed contract references.
4. Open Markets and show the USDC/EURC swap path.
5. Execute or preview a small USDC to EURC swap through the LumenFi pool.
6. Open Liquidity Pool and show pool reserves, LP position, add liquidity, and remove liquidity controls.
7. Open Lending Market and show collateral, debt, available borrow, health, deposit, borrow, repay, and withdraw controls.
8. Open Bridge and explain the USDC onboarding surface and Circle App Kit integration path.
9. Open Docs / Submission / Whitepaper to show the project is documented and ready for review.

## What to emphasize

- LumenFi is not only a UI mockup: it has deployed Arc Testnet contracts.
- The stablecoin pool has active reserves and a working quote.
- LendingPool lists USDC and EURC and enforces LTV / health checks.
- Repo is public, licensed, and includes contract tests.
- The roadmap points toward an Arc Blueprints-powered assistant layer, but current live features are clearly separated from future work.
